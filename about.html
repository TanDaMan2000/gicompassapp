About the product

How GI Compass turns scattered symptoms into a clearer clinical signal.

GI Compass is a pure AI platform with two connected views: a patient view for symptom uploads and a professional view for colonoscopy and endoscopy image uploads. The system analyzes both streams together to produce clearer early signals.
